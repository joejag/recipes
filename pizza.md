Ingredients
===========

* 740g bread flour
* 428g water
* 2t yeast
* 2t salt
* 52g veg oil
* 30g sugar

Method
======

* Raise yeast with water and sugar
* Water (half boil, half tap)
* Add salt
* Mix flour
